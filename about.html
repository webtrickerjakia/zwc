We’re Rishi and Pawan, the two friends behind Zero Waste Club. We’re here to create ethical, sustainable, vegan and planet-friendly daily essentials. We want to be the planet’s most open, ethical, sustainable goods company — and to be radically transparent about how we do it.

So you’ll find no greenwashing here, just beautifully-made things that’ll help play a small part in unf*cking the world. Welcome to Zero Waste Club.

We want a Zero Waste world.

Do enough of the little things right and they add up to big changes. That’s what we’re counting on, what we believe in – and why Zero Waste Club exists.

Because enough’s enough, when it comes to the amount of crap in the world. And we want to play our own small part in turning the planet around.

So in everything we do, we show our workings. We’re open about what goes into the things we make, and radically transparent at every step of the production process.

Want to see how much water we use? Worried about fair pay and working conditions? Us too, and we’ve got the receipts to prove it.
